Apple Pie Recipe

This was my grandmother's apple pie recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste.
  3. Add water, white sugar, and brown sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce the temperature to simmer.
  4. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly.
  5. Cover with a lattice work crust and gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes at 425°F, then reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C).
  7. Continue baking for 35–45 minutes, until the apples are soft.
